Methodology

Revenue Projection: Actual daily revenue from Supabase is summed for elapsed trading days. Remaining days are projected using a blend of MA10 (last 10 days) and quarter average, weighted by completion %.

Expense Model: OLS regression on 8 quarters of actuals: Expenses = Fixed + Variable% x Revenue. Q4 (Mar quarter) has a +15 Cr seasonal adjustment for annual audit/provisions.

PAT: Revenue - Expenses = PBT, then apply effective tax+depreciation rate (~20%) derived from historical actuals.

Confidence Bands: Uncertainty = 2% + 18% x (1 - completion). Narrows from ~20% at quarter start to ~2% at quarter end.

Data Source: Quarterly actuals from Screener.in (MCX consolidated results). Daily revenue from MCX exchange relay + historical API.
